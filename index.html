Clinical Specialties

Specialized Physical Therapy Services

Each service is delivered with the same standard: direct one-on-one care from Dr. Patel, a thorough evaluation, and a plan built precisely around your goals and lifestyle.

01

Pelvic Floor Therapy

Specialized, discreet care for pelvic floor dysfunction conditions such as incontinence, pelvic organ prolapse, and/or pelvic pain — delivered with expertise and compassion.

Pelvic Health for All Genders
02

Orthopedic Rehabilitation

Comprehensive treatment for joint pain, muscle injuries, ligament sprains, and post-surgical recovery — including the McKenzie Method (MDT) for evidence-based assessment and treatment, alongside manual therapy and progressive exercise protocols.

Musculoskeletal
03

Pregnancy & Postpartum Care

Supportive therapy throughout every stage of motherhood — including birth prep, diastasis recti recovery, pelvic girdle pain, and safe return-to-activity after delivery.

Maternal Health
04

Dry Needling

This targeted technique releases trigger points and tight muscle bands to relieve pain, restore function, and accelerate healing.

Specialty Service
05

Manual Therapy

Hands-on techniques to restore mobility, reduce pain, and accelerate recovery. Treatment may include joint mobilization, soft tissue mobilization, myofascial release, cupping therapy, trigger point release, and/or instrument-assisted soft tissue mobilization (IASTM) — selected based on your individual clinical needs.

Hands-On Care
06

Home PT Visits

Receive the same expert-level therapy in the comfort and safety of your home — ideal for post-surgical patients, seniors, or those with transportation limitations.
